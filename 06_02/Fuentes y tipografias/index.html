This means that it can theoretically live forever, as long as it is not killed by predators or other environmental factors. The immortal jellyfish is found in the Mediterranean Sea and the Pacific Ocean. It is a small jellyfish, about the size of a pea, and it has a transparent body with four tentacles. The jellyfish is able to revert back to its polyp stage by transforming its cells into a stem cell-like state. This allows the jellyfish to start its life over again, with a clean slate.
